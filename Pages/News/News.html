Mount Abu Summer Festival

The Mount Abu Summer Festival showcases Rajasthan's vibrant culture with folk dances, music, and boat races on Nakki Lake. Tourists flock to enjoy the cool hill station and its rich traditions during the festivities.

Sirohi Saneshwar Fair

The Sarneshwar Fair in Sirohi celebrates the Rabari community's vibrant traditions with a grand procession led by the Raja of Sirohi. It honors Lord Jagadish, reflecting the district's rich historical and cultural heritage.
